Privacy Policy for Nasoni Roma

Last updated: 3 May 2026

Nasoni Roma is a mobile app that helps users find nearby free public drinking fountains in Rome and open walking navigation to them.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data

The app asks for access to your device location. Location is used to show nearby public drinking fountains, calculate distance to fountains, help open walking navigation, and check whether you are close enough to submit a “Found it” or “Missing” report.

Your live location is processed on your device for map and distance features. When you submit a fountain report, the app may store the approximate distance from the fountain and GPS accuracy value, but it does not store your exact live location as a personal profile.

Fountain Feedback Reports

If you choose to submit feedback such as “Found it” or “Missing,” the app stores a report in Firebase. A report may include the fountain ID, report type, report date, approximate distance from the fountain, approximate GPS accuracy, and device platform.

Anonymous Identifier

The app may use Firebase Anonymous Authentication to create an anonymous user identifier. This is used to limit repeated reports and protect the feedback system from spam. It does not require your name, email address, or account login.

Local App Data

The app may store some information locally on your device, such as whether you have seen the onboarding message and fountains you saved as favorites.

Advertising Data

Nasoni Roma uses Google AdMob to show ads. Google AdMob may collect or use certain information such as device identifiers, ad interaction data, approximate location, usage data, and diagnostic information to provide, measure, and improve ads.

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2. How We Use Information

We use information to show nearby drinking fountains, calculate distance and walking time estimates, open navigation, save user preferences, collect fountain status feedback, prevent repeated or abusive feedback reports, show advertisements, and improve app reliability.

3. Third-Party Services

Nasoni Roma may use Firebase, Google AdMob, Apple Maps, and Google Maps. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Feedback reports may be stored in Firebase so the developer can review fountain status and improve app data quality. Advertising data may be processed by Google AdMob according to Google’s policies.

5. Data Retention

Fountain feedback reports may be kept as long as needed to maintain and improve the accuracy of the app. Favorites and onboarding preferences are stored locally on your device and can be removed by deleting the app.

6. User Choices

You can deny location permission, change location permissions in your device settings, delete the app to remove locally stored favorites and preferences, or contact us to request deletion of feedback linked to your anonymous Firebase identifier where technically possible.

7. Children’s Privacy

Nasoni Roma is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect stored data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
